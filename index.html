Hai Robo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 May 2026

Hai Robo (“the app”) is published by KOREIDEA INNOVATIONS PRIVATE LIMITED (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what data the app handles and why. We collect as little as possible and process most data on your device.

1. Account

You sign in with Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google. We receive a unique user ID and, where you allow it, your name and email, used only to create and identify your account. Authentication and account data are handled by our backend provider, Supabase.

2. Voice & microphone

Wake-word detection (“hai robo”) runs entirely on your device; that audio never leaves the phone. When you ask a question, the recognised text (not raw audio) is sent to our backend, which relays it to Google Gemini to generate a reply. We do not sell or use your conversations for advertising.

3. Camera

The front camera is used only for on-device face tracking (so the robot can turn toward you) and to scan a QR code when pairing a Mac. Video is processed live on the device and is not recorded, stored, or uploaded.

4. Location

Location is used only at the moment you ask for local weather or to book a ride, to set your pickup point. It is checked on demand and is not tracked in the background. Weather is provided by Apple WeatherKit.

5. Calendar & Reminders

With your permission, the app reads your calendar to tell you your next event and creates reminders you ask for. This data stays on your device / in your Apple account; we do not copy it to our servers.

6. Your saved data

Saved places, saved orders, expenses, and the facts you ask the app to remember are stored locally on your device and, via Apple iCloud, across your own devices. If you turn on cloud sync, this data is also stored in your account on our backend (Supabase) so it follows you across devices and platforms. You can export or delete it at any time in Settings.

7. Screen-aware features (optional Mac companion)

If you pair the optional Mac companion, a screenshot of your Mac is sent — only when you ask a screen-related question — to generate a relevant answer. Screenshots are processed for that request and not retained for any other purpose.

8. Service providers

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, WeatherKit, iCloud.
  • Google — Sign in with Google; Gemini (AI replies, via our backend); Maps Places (address lookup).
  • Supabase — authentication, the AI proxy, memory, and optional data sync.

AI provider keys are held only on our backend and are never shipped inside the app.

Cross-border transfer: our service providers (Supabase, Google) may process and store data on servers outside India. By using the app you consent to this transfer, which we make using appropriate safeguards.

9. Consent

When you sign in, we show you what we collect and why, and you consent by signing in. Your consent is limited to the purposes described here. You can withdraw it at any time (see your rights below); withdrawal does not affect processing already carried out.

10. Data retention & deletion

We keep your data only while your account is active or as needed to provide the app. You can delete all on-device data from Settings → Data & privacy, and permanently delete your account and all server-side data from Settings → Account → Delete account. Server-side copies and backups are purged within 30 days of deletion.

11. Your rights

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (and comparable laws), you can:

  • Access a copy of your data — Settings → Data & privacy → Export.
  • Correct / update your data — edit it in the app, or email us.
  • Erase your data — Settings → Data & privacy → Clear all my data, or Settings → Account → Delete account for everything.
  • Withdraw consent — Settings → Account → Sign out, or stop using the app.
  • Grievance redressal — contact our Grievance Officer (below).

12. Children

Hai Robo is intended for users 18 and older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

13. Security & breach

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures (TLS in transit, access controls, provider-side encryption at rest) to protect your data. If a personal-data breach occurs, we will notify affected users and the relevant authority as required by law.
